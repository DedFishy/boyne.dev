About Me

I'm Boyne (aka DedFishy), a high school sophomore from the United States. I enjoy programming, playing video games, biking, driving, hiking, cliff jumping, retro game collecting, and probably some other stuff that I forgot.

I enjoy playing Lethal Company, Helldivers II, Minecraft, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, and Super Mario Bros. Wonder.

The coolest places I've visited are Yellowstone National Park, the Grand Canyon, Cozumel, Puerto Costa Maya, Belize City, and Roatan.

I've been in FIRST robotics since middle school. I'm an alumni of FTC 10062, a mentor of FTC 10062 and FTC 15726, and a current member of FRC 7826.

I'm a member of HackClub and attended Wonderland.

I'm a Protestant Christian and help my church with sermon uploading and distribution with a custom podcast integration system.

Thanks for paying my page a visit!
